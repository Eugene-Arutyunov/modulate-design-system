Configurations

Define where and for whom specific behaviors are detected, and what actions to take. Each rule selects a conversation type, participant roles, one or more behaviors, and optional actions. Rules allow you to focus detection and automate responses without changing the underlying behavior detectors.

Name Description
Institutional Fraud Detection Fraud detection for customer service calls including vishing/phishing and Return fraud
Consumer Fraud Detection Protection for consumers from vishing/phishing in outreach calls
Moderation Content moderation for social conversations including harassment, threats, and safety violations
Interview Monitoring Comprehensive monitoring for hiring interviews to ensure professional conduct, detect bot interviewees, and prevent manipulation attempts
Sales Effectiveness Sales performance monitoring and coaching for intro, demo, and followup calls

Behaviors

Configure what behaviors to detect in conversations such as interruptions, active listening, or emotional responses.

Behavior Description
Mysoginy Evidence of cultural attitudes that propagate systems of marginalizing people based on their gender identity or gender presentation.
Racism Evidence of cultural attitudes that propagate systems of marginalizing people based on their racial or national identity if that identity isn't white.
Homophobia Evidence of cultural attitudes that propagate systems of marginalizing people based on their sexuality.
Transphobia Evidence of cultural attitudes that propagate systems of marginalizing people based on their trans identity.
Sizism Evidence of cultural attitudes that propagate systems of marginalizing people who identify as fat, have a unique or nontraditional body shape, or experience medical conditions that affect their weight.

Rules

Define detection rules by combining conversation types, participant roles, metrics, triggers, and actions into targeted monitoring policies.

Name Description
Customer Service Inbound support calls between customers and service agents
Sales Outreach Outbound calls from sales representatives to prospects
Hiring Interview Structured interviews between recruiters and candidates
Social Media DM Direct messages on social platforms between users
Role Description
Agent Customer-facing support or sales representative
Customer End user or consumer initiating contact
Supervisor Team lead monitoring or joining active conversations
Bot Automated participant in the conversation
Metric Description
Sentiment Score Overall emotional tone of the conversation, from negative to positive
Talk-to-Listen Ratio Proportion of speaking time per participant
Interruption Rate Frequency of one participant cutting off another
Resolution Time Duration from first message to conversation close
Trigger Description
Behavior Detected Fires when a configured behavior is identified in the conversation
Threshold Exceeded Fires when a metric crosses a defined threshold value
Keyword Match Fires when a specific word or phrase appears in the transcript
Conversation End Fires once when the conversation is closed or timed out
Action Description
Send Alert Notify a supervisor or team via webhook, email, or in-app alert
Flag Conversation Mark the conversation for manual review in the audit queue
Log Event Write a structured event to the analytics data stream
Escalate Transfer the conversation to a senior agent or supervisor queue
