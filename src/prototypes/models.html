Music & Speech Detection

Velma-2's music and speech detection models classify audio at the frame level (~192ms resolution), returning independent music and speech probabilities for every frame. Useful for content moderation, ad-break detection, podcast segmentation, and audio analytics — available as both a low-latency batch API and a real-time streaming WebSocket.
