Velma

Our flagship conversation-understanding audio-native model. It analyzes the call itself, not just a transcript.

Its core feature is behaviors: describe what to watch for in plain language, and Velma flags each instance with a confidence score, its reasoning, and the clips it fired on. It ships with 146 behavior templates across safety, compliance, and customer experience. Edit them or write your own.

Every call also returns a diarized transcript, the conversation type, each speaker's role, topics with per-speaker sentiment, and a summary. It detects emotion, accent, deepfakes, and PII/PHI.

Available as a batch REST API and a real-time streaming WebSocket.
