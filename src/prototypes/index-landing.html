Every layer of every conversation

Velma models audio as layered signal — from raw acoustics to high-level intent. Each layer is available to you, and you can configure what matters to your business.

Transcription

Multilingual speech-to-text across 70+ languages. Robust to overlap, noise, accents, and real-world conditions.

Diarization

Emotion

Behaviors
